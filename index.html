Professional training programs

CreativeEdge™ training programs are designed by industry Professionals and tailor-made to suite everyone’s requirements by building strong foundations and enhancing proficiency skills

Film and broadcast design

Designed for individuals with a passion for filmmaking and visual effects aiming to further their careers or gain professional experience in the industry. The program teaches creation of high-end animations, techniques used in the industry like 2D & 3D tracking, keying, compositing and film post-production effects.

Design for print and ePublishing

Designed for students with creative skills to achieve high-end graphics and layout capabilities, backed up by a solid foundation in printing process. The students will benefit from the integration of professional techniques required in designing for print.

New media & interactive design

Designed with the aim of developing professional skills in website designing and authoring interactive business presentations. The students will also benefit from the creative techniques learnt in integrating the images, vector animations, sound and video for a commercial marketing package.

Creative audio and music production

Designed to develop recording and music creation skills through a series of projects that would prepare them to integrate creative music with graphics using the tools and techniques for music/audio creation, production and processing for industry-level practices.

Advanced 3D Design & Concepts

Designed for individuals with a passion for filmmaking and visual effects aiming to further their careers or gain professional experience in the industry. The program teaches creation of high-end animations, techniques used in the industry like 2D & 3D tracking, keying, compositing and film post-production effects.

HDSLR Film Making

Hybrid DSLRs have revolutionised film making. These seminars and workshops are tailor made for participant to understand film making workflows with DSLRs. Participants will get strong foundations of the technology used in this method of filmmaking and will be able to understand and overcome certain limitation in this cost effective filmmaking process
